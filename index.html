Crowder

David Wallace "Crowder" is an American Contemporary Christian singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, and author.

Life

David Crowder was born November 29, 1971 in Texarkana, TX. As a child,, Crowder always had a love for music and found himself dancing along to the beats of Elvis, Willie Nelson, Olivia Newton John, and Bill Gaither. He was attending Baylor University when his musical career began. Baylor University is a Christian college and David was surprised to find out that many students were not attending church. He decided he would try and change this, therefore, the University Baptist Church was born in 1995. The church consisted of 20 members in the beginning, but quickly grew. He went on to form the David Crowder Band and was very successful until retiring in 2012. He knew that God still had plans for his musical career and he made the decision to start writing songs and singing again. This time he would perform solo. Crowder released his first debut solo studio album on May 27, 2014, Neon Steeple with many more to follow. He has had huge success in life with a net worth of about 6 million and now resides in Atlanta, GA with his wife of 30 years, Toni.
